Location Highlights

A Region of Immense Growth Potential

Unmatched Connectivity

Strategic Positioning: Nestled in the newly formed Yadadri Bhuvanagiri District, Aleru is poised for significant growth and development.

Cultural Corridor

Situated between the revered Yadadri Temple and the historic Kolanupaka Jain Temple, the location offers profound cultural and religious significance.

Proposed Metro Expansion

Plans for a Metro extension to Yadadri are set to revolutionize local transportation, offering swift and efficient travel options.

Educational Advancements

The proposed establishment of a Kendriya Vidyalaya School at Gowraypalli will provide residents with access to quality education.

Temple City Corridor

The development of the Temple City Corridor, encompassing Yadadri, Kolanupaka, and ISKCON, is set to attract tourists and pilgrims worldwide.

Industrial Corridor

The forthcoming industrial corridor between Hyderabad and Warangal presents significant potential for industrial and commercial investments.
