Location Highlights

Unparalleled Connectivity

  • Situated along NH-44, providing swift access to Hyderabad and Bangalore
  • Upcoming high-speed Hyderabad-Bangalore bullet train route to further enhance connectivity
  • Benefiting from the MMTS train service extension from Shamsabad to Mahbubnagar
  • Proximity to Burgul MMTS Railway Station near the Outer Ring Road (ORR) ensures seamless local commuting
  • Located within the Shadnagar Municipality and close to its railway station, offering easy access to urban amenities
  • Positioned in the Aviation zone of Hyderabad along the Samshabad-Shadnagar corridor for enhanced travel and logistical benefits

Tech and Industrial Synergy

  • Close to major data centers such as Amazon and Microsoft, catering to a tech-centric lifestyle
  • Direct link road access to multinational giants like Proctor & Gamble and Johnson & Johnson
  • Hosts the world's largest pharmaceutical SEZ, offering significant future investment benefits
  • Special status for the Hyderabad-Karnataka region enhances growth prospects in Gulbarga, Yadadri, Raichur, Koppal, and Bellary

Global Attractions & Cultural Richness

  • Home to the world's second-largest spiritual city, Kanha Shanti Vanam, and the largest meditation temple globally
  • Future site for Asia's proposed largest amusement park
  • The majestic Statue of Equality, symbolizing cultural harmony

Rapid Development & Economic Growth

  • Proximity to the National Remote Sensing Center and ongoing infrastructure developments
  • Equidistant to IT, hardware, industrial, and pharma corridors, providing diverse career opportunities
  • Projected population increase of 8 lakhs within a 10 km radius
  • Addition of 50,000 direct plus 2 lakh indirect employment opportunities, ensuring a thriving community
