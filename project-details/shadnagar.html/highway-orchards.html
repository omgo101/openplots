Location Highlights

Unparalleled Connectivity & Transportation

The Highway Orchards is strategically located with excellent connectivity options:

  • NH-44 Connectivity: Directly connected to NH-44, ensuring swift access to Hyderabad and Bangalore via a well-developed national highway
  • Hyderabad-Bangalore Bullet Train Corridor: Proposed high-speed rail route will enhance connectivity
  • MMTS Train Service Extension: Shamshabad to Mahbubnagar MMTS train service ensures seamless transportation
