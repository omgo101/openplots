Location Highlights

Prime Location & Connectivity

Shadnagar is emerging as a prime real estate destination due to its excellent infrastructure and connectivity. True Gold & Hitek Park offers seamless access to major highways, rail networks, and upcoming transportation projects:

  • Strategic NH-44 Location – Direct access to the 8-lane Hyderabad–Bangalore Expressway
  • MMTS Train Service Extension – Ongoing expansion from Shamshabad to Mahbubnagar
  • Outer Ring Road (ORR) Proximity – Easy access to Hyderabad's ORR
  • Hyderabad–Bangalore Bullet Train Project – Proposed high-speed rail link
  • Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (RGIA) – Just 30-40 minutes away
  • Located in Hyderabad's Aviation Zone – Potential for enhanced economic activity

Shadnagar is not just about connectivity; it is a well-planned region witnessing impressive infrastructural growth:
