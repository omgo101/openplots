Tellapur - Hyderabad's Emerging Residential Hub

Tellapur is a rapidly developing suburb located on the western periphery of Hyderabad, within the Sangareddy district of Telangana. Positioned approximately 5 kilometers from the bustling IT and financial district of Gachibowli, Tellapur has emerged as a sought-after residential locale, especially among professionals working in Hyderabad's tech hubs.
