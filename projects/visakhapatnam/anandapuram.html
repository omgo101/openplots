Anandapuram's Open Plot Market

Anandapuram, located in Visakhapatnam District, Andhra Pradesh, is emerging as a prominent real estate destination. Its strategic location on the Visakhapatnam-Srikakulam highway (NH-16), rapid urbanization, and infrastructure developments like the upcoming Bhogapuram International Airport make it an attractive hub for residential and commercial investments. With relatively lower prices compared to central Visakhapatnam and significant appreciation potential, Anandapuram offers excellent opportunities for investors.
