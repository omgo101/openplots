Bhogapuram's Open Plot Market

Bhogapuram, situated in Vizianagaram district, Andhra Pradesh, is emerging as a key hotspot for real estate development driven by the upcoming Bhogapuram International Airport. Located approximately 40 km from Visakhapatnam, the area benefits from excellent connectivity through national highways and proximity to the Vizag-Chennai Industrial Corridor. With relatively low land costs compared to Visakhapatnam, Bhogapuram is gaining popularity among developers, investors, and homebuyers. The Andhra Pradesh government plans to develop Bhogapuram into a smart city with world-class infrastructure, making it an ideal choice for early movers in the Andhra Pradesh real estate market.
