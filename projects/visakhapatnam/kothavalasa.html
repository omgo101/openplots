Kothavalasa's Real Estate Market

Kothavalasa, located in the Vizianagaram district of Andhra Pradesh, is emerging as a key real estate destination. Strategically positioned around 30 km from Visakhapatnam, it serves as a critical link between Vizag, Vizianagaram, and surrounding regions. Known for its scenic surroundings and serene environment, Kothavalasa is gaining popularity for residential and investment opportunities. With the growth of Visakhapatnam as an IT and industrial hub, Kothavalasa has become an attractive location for affordable housing and real estate development. Its proximity to major employment zones, coupled with ongoing infrastructure improvements, makes it a promising area for real estate growth.
